Tip 1: Review Privacy Settings

Regularly review and adjust your social media privacy settings to control who can see your posts and information.

Tip 2: Be Cautious with Personal Details

Avoid sharing sensitive information like your address, phone number, or financial details on social media.

Tip 3: Limit Public Sharing

Share personal posts only with trusted friends and avoid making everything public.
